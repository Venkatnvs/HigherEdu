SCHOLARSHIPS

Exploring scholarship opportunities is a crucial aspect of planning for your higher education journey. Each university or institute has its unique policies regarding scholarships and financial assistance, aiming to support students in various ways. These opportunities are typically awarded based on factors such as merit, financial need, place of origin, program, and gender.

Here's a breakdown of the different types of scholarships and financial help available:

  • Merit-based: Awarded to students who have demonstrated excellence in academics, sports, extracurricular activities, or volunteering work. These scholarships recognize and reward outstanding achievements.
  • Need-based: Designed to provide financial support to students with limited financial resources. The assessment is usually done by the institute or university to identify those who require assistance to pursue their education.
  • Country-based: Some universities, governments, or public agencies establish agreements to provide financial help or scholarships to students from specific nations. This fosters international collaboration and helps students from those countries pursue education abroad.
  • Program-Specific: Tailored for students who have chosen a particular field of study. These scholarships encourage specialization in specific areas and provide support to those dedicated to advancing their knowledge in a specific discipline.
