Cost of Undergraduate And Postgraduate Degree in New Zealand

The cost of Undergraduate Degree for International Students in New Zeleand varies between NZD 22,000 to NZD 32,000 per year.

The cost of Postgraduate Degree for International Students in New Zeleand varies between NZD 26,000 to NZD 37,000 per year.
